Build now vs. holding company — representative output

Build the site that gets you hired now — not the full holding company. Six polished divisions with nothing shipped reads as hollow and pulls energy from landing actual work.

So: a founder brand now, growing into the holding company structure once the divisions genuinely exist — exactly how the Virgin model scaled (one business made to work, then the next).

Concretely, the three mini-hubs are SMOALD AI, SMOALD Learn and SMOALD Store, and your portfolio sits under that parent brand so clients meet a founder. Keep it a focused hub-and-spoke; add spokes as real products ship.
